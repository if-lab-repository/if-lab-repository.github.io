Selection Criteria

Researchers applying for attending the workshop (except for the invited talks) will be selected for funding based on the following (ranked) criteria:

1. Presenters at the workshop. Directorate for Engineering

2. Lack of any available travel support.

3. Membership in an underrepresented group.

During the speaker invitation process, the organizing committee will also give a high priority to members in underrepresented groups. One of the four thrust leaders is a female researcher, Sharon Hu from Notre Dame. Our goal is to ensure at least 20% of the speakers are in an underrepresented group.

Reporting

Researchers that are supported by this grant for attending the workshop will be required to submit a summary of their experience at or right after the workshop. A summary of these experiences will be included in the final report.
